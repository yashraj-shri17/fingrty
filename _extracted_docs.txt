Fingrity Advisors LLP - Brand Identity Guide
1. Logo Overview
The fing₹ity logo represents the firm's core philosophy — financial integrity with global reach. It combines modern design simplicity with symbolic depth, blending professionalism and approachability. It reflects the firm's commitment to precision, trust, and international perspective.
2. Logo Composition
• Wordmark: “fingrity” in lowercase communicates accessibility and modernity.
• The letter ‘r’ is replaced with the Indian Rupee symbol (₹), rendered in teal, symbolizing:
   - Financial expertise and domain mastery.
   - Indian roots and credibility.
   - Precision and value creation.
3. Colour Palette
Primary Text (fing…ity): Deep Navy Blue (#0F2B46)
Rupee Symbol (₹): Teal (#2AA6A0)
Background: White (#FFFFFF)
4. Font Family
Typeface: Poppins / Montserrat
Weight: SemiBold (600)
Kerning: Tight spacing for compact, modern appearance.
5. Symbolism & Concept Meaning
Lowercase Wordmark: Approachability and modernity.
Rupee Symbol (₹): Financial expertise and Indian roots.
Navy Blue: Trust, structure, and expertise.
Teal Accent: Balance and innovation.
White Space: Clarity, transparency, and ethical conduct.
6. Usage Guidelines
• Minimum Clear Space: Height of the ‘f’ around all sides.
• Minimum Size: 35 mm width (print), 250 px width (digital).
• Background: White or light neutral preferred.
• Do Not: Stretch, skew, rotate, or change rupee symbol color.
7. Tagline
“Integrity in Finance. Globally.”
